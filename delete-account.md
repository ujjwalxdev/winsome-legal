# How to Delete Your Winsome Account

If you would like to permanently delete your Winsome account and all associated data, follow the steps below.

---

## Steps to Delete Your Account

1. Open the **Winsome** app on your device.

2. Tap the **Profile** tab (bottom right of the screen).

3. Scroll down to the bottom of the Profile screen.

4. Tap **Delete Account**.

5. A confirmation dialog will appear. Type **DELETE** in the text field to confirm.

6. Tap the **Delete** button.

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted immediately.

---

## What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

- Your name and email address
- All wins you have logged
- Your account credentials and session data
- Your app preferences (dark mode, reminder time, categories)

---

## What Is Not Deleted

- Any data retained by Google as part of Google Sign-In is governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). You can manage your Google account data at [myaccount.google.com](https://myaccount.google.com).

---

## Important Notes

- Deletion is **immediate and irreversible**. There is no grace period or way to recover your account once deleted.
- If you only want to stop using the app temporarily, you can simply log out from the Profile screen instead of deleting your account.
